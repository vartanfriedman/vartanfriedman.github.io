Drum and bass is a genre of electronic dance music characterized by fast breakbeats with heavy bass and sub-bass lines, samples, and synthesizers. The genre grew out of the UK's jungle scene in the early 1990s. The popularity of drum and bass at its commercial peak ran parallel to several other UK dance styles.
